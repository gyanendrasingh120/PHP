Frequently asked questions:
Sign up page says “Please fill in your residential area using the drop down list”. What do I do?

In the Residential area field in the sign up page, fill in the name of your locality, and wait for a list to automatically appear. Choose your locality from that list. That’ll do the job!

Sign up page says “Which area do you stay in in…”?

If you have filled in the name of your city, please put in the name of your locality instead. This helps us provide you with more relevant content within the app.

How does the referral system work?

When you like a product, service or app, you tell your friends about it. And that helps that product grow. If you like PocketAd, and tell a friend about it, it will help us grow. And we want to do our best to thank you for it. So when you tell someone about PocketAd, just feed in his/her phone number in the Referrals section.

We won’t call or text them. We assume you’ve done that already. As soon as your friend joins PocketAd, we’ll reward you with some extra cash as a token of our gratitude.

What do the green ticks and red crosses mean in the Referrals section?

The green tick indicates that we have registered your referral with us and not that the referral has been successful. As soon as the referred number joins PocketAd, you will be rewarded with ₹5, and the number will disappear from your Referrals section, allowing you to refer more people if you wish.

The red cross indicates that the number you are trying to refer has already been referred by someone else before you, or that it has already joined PocketAd.

Can I only refer 5 people through the Referrals section?

Of course not. You can refer as many people as you want. However, at any given moment, you cannot have more than 5 waiting referrals.

If you wish to edit any of the waiting referrals, just double tap on the number, and fill in the new number that you wish to refer.

Does PocketAd work only for prepaid phones?

Of course not! PocketAd works perfectly fine for both prepaid and post-paid mobile users. A user can also do a DTH recharge using PocketAd.

What do I do if I can’t finish a game?

Click on the Quick Access button on the top-right in the Play section, and then click on this button . If 15 seconds have passed since the game started, you will get a new game to play.

How do I recharge my phone?

Go to the Redeem section. If you wish to do a prepaid recharge, carefully fill in the phone number and the mobile network details, and write in the amount of recharge that you wish to do. The amount must not exceed the cash available in your PocketAd wallet. If you wish to do a postpaid bill adjustment, go to the Postpaid tab on top, and fill In your details there.

Once you are done filling in the details, click on the Recharge/Pay Bill button. You will be asked to key in your password. Fill that in, and we’ll do the rest.

Why am I not getting money for playing games?

That’s because you’ve earned your daily share of money. We maintain a daily limit on how much money you can win by playing games, and you can see that in the My Account section. So, if you win your daily share of money, and still have lives left, you will not win any money even if you win a game.

How much money can I win in a month?

The monthly or daily limit on the amount of money you can win may change from time to time. You can always see what the limit is from the Wallet tab in the My Account section.

Can I transfer my cash to someone else?

No, you cannot transfer PocketAd cash between users, but you can always do a recharge on their phone number or DTH card using your own PocketAd cash.

What is my password used for?

It is very important that you keep your password secure. It helps us keep your money secure. You will need to key in your password before completing any redemption. This ensures that your PocketAd cash can only be used by you.

How do I choose which game I get to play?

You cannot choose which games you get to play. However, if you really enjoy a game, you can contact us, and we'll do something about it.

What is the cost of using PocketAd?

PocketAd doesn’t charge you anything. The only charges you incur are the data charges that result from the download of games, which depend on your carrier, internet connection type, etc.

My Account shows that some amount has already, been redeemed on a previous installation. What does that mean?

If you have bought a second hand phone, or signed up after uninstalling the app, this message may be visible. It means that within that month, a previous user had collected and redeemed that much money. This amount, subtracted from the total monthly limit, is the amount you can win in that given month.

I am connected to Wi-Fi, but PocketAd is asking me to check my internet connection and try again.

Sometimes, our devices are connected to Wi-Fi networks, or our data connections are on, but due to certain errors, they cannot access the Internet. If you are connected to a Wi-Fi network, and are still getting that message, trying restarting your Wi-Fi router. If you are on a data connection, put off your data connection, and on it again. It should work fine.

If you can access other websites on that connection, but PocketAd displays the error message, please contact PocketAd Support at support@pocketad.co.in.

Is my device supported?

All Android devices above Android version 4.0 support PocketAd. To check you Android version, go to your device’s Settings>About Phone>Android Version.

I am using a Xiaomi handset, and my phone number is not being verified.

Some devices may experience a glitch while verifying the phone number during the sign up process. PocketAd is working with these manufacturers to resolve the issues. Please bear with us.
