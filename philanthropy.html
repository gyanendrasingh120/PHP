Bachpan Bachao Andolan

Nobel laureate Kailash Satyarthi-led Bachpan Bachao Andolan (BBA) is India’s largest grassroots movement for the protection of children. BBA has rescued more than 80,000 victims of trafficking, slavery, and child labour and has helped them re-establish promising futures for themselves.

Bachpan Bachao Andolan

Bachpan Bachao Andolan (BBA) symbolizes India’s largest grassroots movement for the protection of children, ensuring their quality education. By 2013, BBA had rescued more than 82800 victims of trafficking, slavery and child labour and has helped them re-establish trust in society and find promising futures for themselves.
